How the event app works

One simple app guides every guest through the day — and lets your team run the whole room from a single screen. No app store, no downloads.

For your guests

On their own phone

  1. 1Sign inName, email, and what they'd like to get out of the day. A seat's assigned for them.
  2. 2Navi says helloA friendly guide greets them by name and always shows the one next thing to do.
  3. 3Get a heads-upTurn on phone alerts for game time, buffet, and the next talk — even with the app closed.
  4. 4Play the gameA quick, fun group game with a live leaderboard.
  5. 5Take home a recapA short, personal summary of each talk — one tap to share on WhatsApp.

For your team

One control screen

  1. 1UnlockA private passcode keeps the controls with your team only.
  2. 2Steer the dayMove everyone from welcome, to seated, to buffet with a tap. Every phone updates live.
  3. 3Send a nudgePing every guest's phone with a reminder in one tap.
  4. 4Run the gameStart it, spring a surprise, crown the winner — and the whole room celebrates.
  5. 5See who's inA live guest list with scores, ready to download.
